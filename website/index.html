Welcome to News Interaction Research by Berkeley

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this study conducted by UC Berkeley. This study is designed to understand how people interact with news articles and how they perceive the credibility of news sources.

Instructions

Welcome back to our study! Our study aims at understanding the different outcomes when a news recommender system is attached with different features. You will need to participate in the experiments by following these 6 steps:

  1. Conduct a pre-survey on Qualtrics.
  2. Input your Prolific ID to get your unique Gmail Account, Password, and backup recovery mail address.
  3. Install a chrome extension. The extension is only guaranteed to function correctly on Google Chrome. So we require that you must have Google Chrome installed and participate in our study using Google Chrome. If you don't have Google Chrome installed, you can download it via this link: https://www.google.com/chrome/. If you do not use Google Chrome, you might be unable to finish the experiments.
  4. Browse google news, click on the news you want to read for several iterations. Notice: Every click within 5 seconds will not be counted as a valid click.
  5. Conduct an end-survey on Qualtrics.
  6. Conduct a follow-up survey on Qualtrics.
