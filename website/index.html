Welcome to News Interaction Research by Berkeley

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this study conducted by UC Berkeley. This study is designed to understand how people interact with news articles and how they perceive the credibility of news sources. If you have any questions about the study, please contact the researcher at help@haasresearch.org.

Consent

By clicking the button below, you are agreeing to participate in this study. You are also agreeing that you are at least 18 years old and that you have read and understood the information provided in the consent form. After you click the button, you will be redirected to a pre-study survey to collect some basic information about you. This survey will be conducted on the Qualtrics platform. After completing the survey, you will provided a completion code and be redirected back here.
