Welcome to our Research

We appreciate your participation in this study. Our research examines patterns in news consumption. We want to emphasize that it is required for participants to use Google Chrome, as the study is optimized for this browser. We are excited to have you on board and look forward to your participation.

Instructions

Your participation in the experiments will require the following six steps:

  1. Please carry out a pre-survey using Qualtrics.
  2. Enter your Prolific ID to receive your unique Gmail Account, Password, and backup recovery email address.
  3. Please install the following extension. This extension is only guaranteed to work optimally on Google Chrome, hence we mandate its usage for participation in our study. If Google Chrome is not installed on your device, you can download it using this link: https://www.google.com/chrome. Please note, failure to use Google Chrome may prevent you from completing the experiments.
  4. Browse through Google News, selecting the articles you wish to read. Please note: any clicks within a short time interval will not be considered as valid.
  5. Please conduct a final survey on Qualtrics.
  6. Please conduct a follow-up survey using Qualtrics 30 days later.

Upon completing the survey, you will receive a completion code. Please enter this identifier accurately into the text box below to proceed to the subsequent sections.

Should you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us at help@haasresearch.org.
